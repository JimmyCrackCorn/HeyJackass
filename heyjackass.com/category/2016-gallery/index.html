Police Chase

A police chase that began in Tinley Park ended violently in Chicago on Friday, July 1st, when a car full of alleged armed robbers crashed into a police SUV, injuring eight people including four police officers. Two of the suspects later died. View on YouTube. Video by @SPOTNEWSonIG.
